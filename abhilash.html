Abhilash Mishra

CEO of NSE Academy Ltd

Abhilash Misra is the CEO of NSE Academy Ltd. (A wholly owned subsidiary of National Stock Exchange Ltd.) and also Board Director at TalentSprint. In his previous assignment he was Director-Global Outreach and Executive Education for India, Middle East and South Asia regions at University of Chicago Booth School of Business. He is an award winning Banking and Financial Services business leader with 25+ years of extensive operating, sales and marketing exposure at AXA Investment Managers, Standard Chartered Bank, ICICI Bank and Aditya Birla Group. He is an expert at running Joint Venture alliances, has set up 3 business units, managed 2 M&A integrations, implemented multiple turn around annual operating plans and lean business strategies. He has an MBA from The University of Chicago, Booth School of Business and is a Marshal Goldsmith Certified Executive Coach.
