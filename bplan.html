About

The contest is based on presenting an idea for a startup in front of a panel of experts and convince them to invest your company ( simulating the meetings with VCs and Angel Investors). Apart from the prizes, the winners will be allowed to incubate their idea at the ROLTA incubation centre under the guidance of the E-Cell.
There will be three phases to this competition:
Learning Phase: A guest/speaker will explain how to make a lean canvas and a pitch deck.
Preliminary Phase: Teams will submit their idea by making a lean canvas.
Online Phase: The teams with the best idea will be invited to pitch their ideas to the panel of experts. Each team will be given 7 minutes followed by a 3 minute Q&A session.
