About

Stock Trading Simulation provides a risk-free environment with a realistic replica of the Dalal Street to try and learn relevant skills needed for trading before they trade in real life, with cash rewards for the top traders. Participants will have two sessions consisting of a 2-day guided workshop with supply of study material that familiarizes them with the basic terms and advanced techniques of Stock Investing by the guest trainer, thereafter Keynote session by the Guest of Honour. The Stock Market Simulation Event will take place for 5 days and the participant with maximum net worth by the end of the contest wins.

• Timeline

8-9 January 2022
Training Workshop

Master advanced techniques of Stock Trading including Technical Analysis, Intraday Trading and many more in this masterclass with a Full-Time Trader by profession.

10-14 January 2022
Stock Trading Simulation

Compete with the smart minds to win cash rewards and exciting prizes by earning maximum net profit in the virtual simulation of Stock Trading.

17th January 2022
Keynote Session

Get your questions answered, as well as tips and tricks to earn maximum gains from the volatile Dalal Street with an expert on Stock Trading.

  • Top 50 traders - Courses worth INR 5000

    • • Eligibility

  • Participants registered for the workshop are eligible for the contest.
  • Every member participating must be an Undergraduate student or below.

    • • Judging Criteria

  • Net profit gained at the end of the event.

    • • Rules and Regulations

  • Participants can register themselves via the official E-Cell NIT-B website.
  • The Simulation game will begin on 10 January 2022, on the website of Finlatics and will last for 5 days.
  • All the required links will be provided to only registered participants.
  • Each participant will be given a base of 1 Lacs INR (virtual money cannot be withdrawn) at the start of the event that they will be trading with.
  • Stocks of around 100 companies will be added while creating the game. The companies may be taken from NSE, BSE or from other stock exchanges of cities of India.
  • Coordinators from E-Cell will keep track of the assets of all participants and will make sure that no fallacy occurs.
  • In case of any dispute, the decision by the Event Coordinator, passed NOT without consultation and consent of the team, will be final.
  • At the start of the event, 10 January 2022; the value of the stocks will be their actual value at the moment; as the trading proceeds, the values of stocks will rise or fall according to the actions of the traders and will be accommodated suitably by the website itself.
  • At the conclusion of the event, 14 January 2022; the net worth of all participants will be calculated by summing up the price of their stocks and the liquid cash at their disposal.
  • The top three traders will be announced and awarded at the closing ceremony of E-Summit’22. The cash money will be sent to them via online payment.
