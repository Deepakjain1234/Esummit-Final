About

The participants are given 48 hours to develop a prototype and pitch it to judges, angel investors, and venture capitalists. The goal of this is to encourage our hackers to explore the unconventional. By going away from the ordinary, participants will develop breakthroughs that will change the world for the better.

Eligibility

The teams registering for the hackathon must meet the following criteria to be eligible for participation

  • Every team must have at most 4 – 5 members
  • Every member of the team must be a college student.

    • Rules and regulation

    1. Themes will be provided to all teams.
    2. The team needs to pick up one theme and build upon it.
    3. The solution can be related to any domain shared.
    4. Team leaders must create a common GitHub repository, and all other team members will only push their code on that.
    5. All team members must share their GitHub profile link with us, and team leaders must share their project repository.
    6. Teams can take help from the internet if they are stuck, but they must use their creativity and skills to develop a unique solution.
    7. Teams can use any framework or library at their convenience.
    8. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited; the solution of all teams will be strictly monitored, and if found to be copied from other teams or a complete solution from the internet, they will be immediately disqualified.
    9. After completing the project, it must be live (must be hosted on the internet, can use GitHub Pages or Heroku or any free services of cloud).
    10. Teams must also create a PPT and report of their projects & will need to present their project along with the PPT on the day of final judgment
    11. Teams must update their status of projects every 3 hrs in a common WhatsApp group. If they fail to do so, they will be contacted by Event Coordinators. If any team remains inactive for more than 6 hrs, then the team will be disqualified.

    Schedule

    • 18th to 27th January 2022 (10 days)

      Registration

      A participant must register himself with team during interval of 18th to 27th January 2022.

    • 27th January to 6th February 2022 (10 days)

      Idea Submission

      Team members must submit an idea based on the given theme. Accepted responses – PPT, Video, Animation etc.

    • 7th to 8th February 2022 (2 days)

      Shortlisting Teams

      Team members review the submissions, and 20-40 teams are shortlisted. Meanwhile, participants will attend an interactive session/seminar/workshop by our title sponsors (if any).

    • 9th to 10th February 2022 (2 days)

      Round 1 result Announcement

      Round 1 is announced, and WhatsApp groups are formed. The group consisting of participants along with 2 E-Cell Members and a mentor.

    • 11th February 2022

      Rules and Regulations

      Welcome Meet hosted by E-Cell Team Members explaining rules and regulations about the hackathon.

    • 12th to 13th February 2022 (2 days)

      Hackathon

      Participants will be given 2 days of time to design a prototype as desired for given probelm.

    • 14th to 15th February 2022(2 days)

      Pitching Round

      Pitching round before judges panel.

    • 16th February 2022

      Result Announced

      Final verdict of Hackathon will be announced

    Hackathon rules for Participants

  • Registration Round : - The teams have to register and select a problem statement for them.
  • Idea Submission : - The team members have to submit a ppt showcasing their idea to solve the problem statement
  • Hacking Phase:
    1. A Whatsapp group will be made containing participants along with 2 E-Cell Members and a mentor.
    2. Hacking phase will be of 48 hours.

    • Tracks

  • Web Development : - Build web applications using the frameworks and tools
  • Mobile Development: - Develop interactive mobile applications for iOS or Android
  • Artificial Intelligence: - Implement machine learning concepts to solve tasks
  • Prototype designed must be User friendly.
